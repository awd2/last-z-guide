FAQ

How do Alliance Duel chests work?

You earn rewards by reaching score thresholds for Chests 1–9. The thresholds and rewards are listed in the Day 1–5 and Day 6 tables.

What is different on Day 6 (Enemy Buster)?

Day 6 uses different score thresholds compared to Days 1–5, but the same 9 chest structure.

What does “Daily Total” mean?

Daily Total is the sum of all 9 chest rewards for that day.

How is Weekly Total calculated?

Weekly Total is calculated as (Daily Total × 5) + Day 6 total.

How to Read These Tables

  • Chest headers — show thresholds for each chest
  • Reward rows — list items you get per chest
  • Daily/Weekly Totals — summed rewards across all 9 chests
