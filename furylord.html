Furylord Overview

Furylord is a world boss event that appears four times per day and stays active for three hours each time. It does not appear on Sundays.

You can fight solo or with your alliance. Attacks are limited to 4 per day, and only your highest single hit counts toward the daily leaderboard.

Completing all 4 attacks returns fuel cans in the reward track.
