Last Z Farm Account Guide - Setup, Season 2, Resources

Economy Guide • Updated February 1, 2026 • 6 min read

By Last Z Guides

Quick Answer

Farm account setup in 5 steps:

  1. ① Create a new account · separate email + device/emulator
  2. ② Unlock Profile menu · play 5-10 minutes
  3. ③ Account → Character Management · create new character
  4. ④ Select your server · before Season 2
  5. ⑤ Join an alliance · farm alliance or main alliance

ℹ️ Timing: Community reports say farm accounts must be created before Season 2 starts on your server.

What Is a Farm Account?

A farm account is a secondary character used to gather resources and support your main Headquarters (HQ). Players use farm accounts to speed up progression, donate resources, and keep the main account moving faster.

How to Create a Farm Account

Create a new account with a separate email, play until the Profile menu unlocks, then go to Profile → Account → Character Management and select your main server. Finish the tutorial and join an alliance. The full walkthrough is below in Step-by-Step Setup.

Season 2 Rule (Important)

Community reports indicate you can only create new farm accounts on a server before Season 2 starts. If your server is already in Season 2, creating a new character in that same server may be blocked. If you are close to Season 2, create your farm account as early as possible.

What You Need

  • A separate email for the new account
  • A second device or emulator (phone, tablet, or PC). Popular options include LDPlayer or BlueStacks.
  • 5-10 minutes to unlock the Profile menu

Step-by-Step Setup

  1. Install Last Z on a second device or emulator and sign in with a new email.
  2. Play for a few minutes until the Profile icon appears in the top-left.
  3. Open Profile → Account → Character Management.
  4. Select Create New Character, then choose your main server.
  5. Finish the tutorial and join a farm alliance or your main alliance.

After You Create the Farm

  • Keep it active with basic tasks and resource collection.
  • Join an alliance to gain gifts and protection.
  • Support your main HQ by providing resources when needed.
  • Optional: delete the starter character created on another server if you will never use it.

FAQ

Can I create a farm account after Season 2 starts?

Community reports say new farms must be created before Season 2 begins on your server. After Season 2 starts, creating a new character in that state may be blocked.

Do I need a separate email for a farm account?

Yes — using a separate email is the standard way to create another account on the same server.

How do I create a farm account on my server?

Make a new account with a separate email, unlock the Profile menu, then go to Character Management and select your main server.

What should I do after creating the farm account?

Join a farm alliance or your main alliance, keep the account active for resource gains, and use it to support your main Headquarters (HQ).

Game mechanics and account rules may change with updates. This guide was last validated in February 2026.
