Quick Answer

Last Z Field Research total cost: 594,430 badges

  1. ① 14 nodes · attack, defense, training speed, troop HP
  2. ② First 11 nodes · 182,930 badges
  3. ③ Last 3 nodes · 411,500 badges (2/3 of total cost)
  4. ④ Best upgrade · Recharge Shield (+10% troop HP)

⚠️ Warning: The last 3 nodes cost more than the first 11 combined. Plan your badges carefully — don't get trapped at 70% completion.

ℹ️ After completion: Unlocks Shooter Training, Assaulter Training, and Rider Training trees.

Unlock Requirements

How to Unlock Field Research

If you don't see Field Research in your lab, complete these requirements first:

  1. New Home — 100% complete
  2. Elite Troops — 100% complete
  3. Military Strategies — 40% (unlocks Siege to Seize)
  4. Siege to Seize — 100% complete

Only after all four requirements are met, Field Research appears in your lab.

The Hidden Badge Trap

Why Most Players Get Surprised

The first 11 nodes cost 182,930 badges. You progress quickly and think Field Research isn't that expensive.

Then you reach the last 3 nodes. They cost 411,500 badges — more than double the first 11 combined.

Section Badge Cost % of Total
First 11 nodes 182,930 31%
Last 3 nodes 411,500 69%
Total 594,430 100%

The trap: At ~70% completion, you feel committed. The game quietly pushes you toward buying more badges. Plan ahead to avoid this.

What Field Research Unlocks

Three New Research Trees

After completing Field Research, three new trees appear in your lab:

  • Shooter Training — attack, defense, and stats for shooter troops
  • Assaulter Training — attack, defense, and stats for assaulter troops
  • Rider Training — attack, defense, and stats for rider troops

These trees significantly increase stats for your main troop type. Focus on the tree matching your faction (Assaulter for Blood Rose, Shooter for Wings of Dawn, Rider for Guard of Order).

Field Research Tips

  • Plan for 600k badges — don't start if you can't finish
  • Recharge Shield is the best node — +10% troop HP is extremely strong
  • Complete on Day 3 — use badge research during Alliance Duel Age of Science day
  • Don't rush the last 3 nodes — they cost 2/3 of the entire tree
  • Worth it for T10 users — the bonuses greatly improve power and survivability
  • Focus your faction's troop type — after completion, prioritize the matching training tree

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock Field Research?

Complete: New Home 100%, Elite Troops 100%, Military Strategies 40% (to unlock Siege to Seize), then Siege to Seize 100%. Only then Field Research appears in your lab.

How many badges does Field Research cost?

594,430 badges total for all 14 nodes. The first 11 nodes cost 182,930 badges, while the last 3 nodes cost 411,500 badges (more than double).

What is Recharge Shield?

Recharge Shield is the final node in Field Research. It increases troop HP by 10% (levels 1-10) and costs 102,300 badges. This is one of the strongest stats in the game.

What does completing Field Research unlock?

Three new research trees: Shooter Training, Assaulter Training, and Rider Training. These significantly boost stats for specific troop types.

Is Field Research worth it?

Yes, especially for T10 troop users. Total bonuses: +60% infield attack, +60% infield defense, +20% attack/defense per troop type, +10% training speed, +10% troop HP.

Why don't I see Field Research in my lab?

You haven't completed the unlock requirements. Check if you have: New Home 100%, Elite Troops 100%, Military Strategies 40%+, and Siege to Seize 100%.
