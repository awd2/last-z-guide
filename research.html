Research Priority Order

Step 1: Hero Training ‚Üí Cockpit Only

Why: Cockpit gives meaningful hero stat boosts. After Cockpit, you only get ~400 extra ATK/DEF for extremely high badge costs.

Common mistake: Many players max Hero Training thinking it's important. It's a badge trap. Stop at Cockpit and move on.

Step 2: Military Strategies ‚Üí Troop HP

Why: This tree is cheap and provides crucial Troop HP buffs. Focus on the bottom nodes that match your main faction's troop type (Assaulter for Blood Rose, etc.).

Tip: Don't max "Move Out" ‚Äî it's expensive. Upgrade to level 10-20 max.

Step 3: Peace Shield ‚Üí Urgent Rescue

Unlock requirements: 50% Shelter Building + 45% Military Strategies.

Key research: Urgent Rescue reduces unit deaths by up to 20% (10 ranks). This is the direct counter to enemy Annihilation skill. Essential for PvP.

Step 4: Siege to Seize (Situational)

Warning: This tree increases your Destruction Value. Only research if you're ready for more aggressive gameplay.

When to skip: If you prefer defensive play or are in a NAP alliance, delay this tree.

Step 5: UST (Special Unit Training)

Goal: Unlock T10 units ‚Äî the strongest troops in the game.

Cost: ~1.4 million badges total. Takes years for F2P or ~$15,000 to speed up. Farm badges from Lucky Discounter events.

Pro tip: Start UST immediately after Urgent Rescue if you're committed to long-term progression.

Frequently Asked Questions

What research should I prioritize in Last Z (research guide)?

Priority order: Hero Training to Cockpit ‚Üí Military Strategies ‚Üí Peace Shield (Urgent Rescue) ‚Üí Siege to Seize ‚Üí UST. This maximizes combat power while efficiently using badges.

Is Hero Training worth maxing in Last Z research priority?

No. Only research until Cockpit. After that, you get minimal stats (+400 ATK/DEF) for very high badge costs. Those badges are better spent on Military Strategies or saving for UST.

What is Urgent Rescue (Peace Shield research) in Last Z?

Urgent Rescue is a Peace Shield research that reduces unit deaths by up to 20%. It directly counters the enemy's Annihilation skill. Must-have for PvP content.

How many badges do I need for UST (Special Unit Training)?

1.4 million badges for full T10 unlock through UST. This takes years for F2P players. Whales can speed it up for $15,000 USD.

Should I buy the second laboratory in this research guide?

Yes, 100%. It doubles your research speed. One of the best investments in the game. Buy it as soon as you can afford it.

How do I unlock Peace Shield research in Last Z?

Complete or reach certain percentages in current trees. Example: 100% Elite Troops unlocks Military Strategies. 50% Shelter + 45% Military Strategies unlocks Peace Shield.
