Last Z Research Priority Guide — What to Research First

Research Guide • Updated January 2026 • 8 min read

Quick Answer

Optimal research order in Last Z: 1) Hero Training → stop at Cockpit, 2) Military Strategies → Troop HP buffs, 3) Peace Shield → Urgent Rescue (-20% losses), 4) Siege to Seize (optional), 5) UST for T10 units (long-term). Don't max Hero Training — badges are better spent elsewhere. Buy the second laboratory ASAP.

Research Priority Order

Step 1: Hero Training → Cockpit Only

Why: Cockpit gives meaningful hero stat boosts. After Cockpit, you only get ~400 extra ATK/DEF for extremely high badge costs.

Common mistake: Many players max Hero Training thinking it's important. It's a badge trap. Stop at Cockpit and move on.

Step 2: Military Strategies → Troop HP

Why: This tree is cheap and provides crucial Troop HP buffs. Focus on the bottom nodes that match your main faction's troop type (Assaulter for Blood Rose, etc.).

Tip: Don't max "Move Out" — it's expensive. Upgrade to level 10-20 max.

Step 3: Peace Shield → Urgent Rescue

Unlock requirements: 50% Shelter Building + 45% Military Strategies.

Key research: Urgent Rescue reduces unit deaths by up to 20% (10 ranks). This is the direct counter to enemy Annihilation skill. Essential for PvP.

Step 4: Siege to Seize (Situational)

Warning: This tree increases your Destruction Value. Only research if you're ready for more aggressive gameplay.

When to skip: If you prefer defensive play or are in a NAP alliance, delay this tree.

Step 5: UST (Special Unit Training)

Goal: Unlock T10 units — the strongest troops in the game.

Cost: ~1.4 million badges total. Takes years for F2P or ~$15,000 to speed up. Farm badges from Lucky Discounter events.

Pro tip: Start UST immediately after Urgent Rescue if you're committed to long-term progression.

Research Tips from Top Players

  • Buy Second Laboratory — doubles your research speed, essential purchase
  • Align with Capital Buff — reduces research time by 1 hour when active
  • Save stars and speed-ups — use during research events for bonus rewards (see Alliance Duel Day 3)
  • Don't rush Fully Armed Alliance (FAA) — leave it for later, focus on defensive research first
  • Check unlock requirements — plan your path to unlock new trees efficiently

Research Trees Unlock Order

How Trees Unlock

Each research tree unlocks when you complete or reach a certain percentage in previous trees:

  • New Home → unlocks HQ Management
  • 100% Elite Troops → unlocks Military Strategies
  • 50% Shelter + 45% Military Strategies → unlocks Peace Shield

Frequently Asked Questions

What research should I prioritize in Last Z?

Priority order: Hero Training to Cockpit → Military Strategies → Peace Shield (Urgent Rescue) → Siege to Seize → UST. This maximizes combat power while efficiently using badges.

Is Hero Training worth maxing in Last Z?

No. Only research until Cockpit. After that, you get minimal stats (+400 ATK/DEF) for very high badge costs. Those badges are better spent on Military Strategies or saving for UST.

What is Urgent Rescue in Last Z?

Urgent Rescue is a Peace Shield research that reduces unit deaths by up to 20%. It directly counters the enemy's Annihilation skill. Must-have for PvP content.

How many badges do I need for T10 units?

Approximately 1.4 million badges for full T10 unlock through UST. This takes years for F2P players. Whales can speed it up for around $15,000 USD.

Should I buy the second laboratory?

Yes, 100%. It doubles your research speed. One of the best investments in the game. Buy it as soon as you can afford it.

How do I unlock new research trees?

Complete or reach certain percentages in current trees. Example: 100% Elite Troops unlocks Military Strategies. 50% Shelter + 45% Military Strategies unlocks Peace Shield.
