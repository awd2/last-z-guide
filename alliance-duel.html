Last Z Alliance Duel Guide — Day-by-Day F2P Strategy

Alliance Guide • Updated January 2026 • 12 min read

Based on: Top Tips to Dominate Alliance Duel Event and other sources

Quick Answer

Alliance Duel is about saving resources and spending on the right days. You don't need to spend money — just save wrenches, alloys, power cores, and fragments for their specific days. Sync with Full Preparedness events (system time 8 and 20) to score in both events simultaneously. Priority research: Alliance Recognition — it doubles rewards and unlocks chests 7-9.

The Core Strategy

Save Resources, Spend on Right Days

The entire Alliance Duel strategy comes down to:

  • Save — Don't use items randomly throughout the week
  • Sync — Match your spending with Full Preparedness events
  • Stop — Don't chase 6 million if you can't afford it

Full Preparedness Sync

Full Preparedness events run at system time 8 and system time 20 (Echoplus time). During these windows, you score points in BOTH events simultaneously.

Example: On Training day, do your troop training at system time 8 or 20 to get Alliance Duel points AND Full Preparedness rewards.

Priority Research: Alliance Recognition

Why Alliance Recognition First

This is the most important research in the game for F2P players. It doesn't give fighting power, but it:

  • Doubles daily rewards — 240 badges instead of 120, 40k alloys instead of 20k
  • Unlocks chests 7-9 — Access to all Alliance Duel rewards
  • Gives more versatile fragments — 2 orange fragments instead of 1
  • Increases score capacity — Easier to hit 6 million points

Badge Requirements

  • Event King research: ~143,500 badges total
  • Event Expert: Additional 22-44k badges
  • Full max: 175-200k badges total

The investment pays off: Once maxed, you earn 8,000+ badges per week from Alliance Duel alone.

Research Benefits

  • Event King: +200% global score modifier
  • Event Expert: +100% points
  • Combined: 300% extra points when maxed

Day-by-Day Strategy

Day 1 (Monday): Modded Vehicle Boost

What gives points:

  • Using golden wrenches
  • Using modification blueprints
  • Opening vehicle component boxes
  • Opening chip chests (orange = 1 million points!)
  • Radar tasks
  • Rally boomers

Strategy:

  • Save radar tasks from Sunday
  • Open ALL component boxes and chip chests on Monday only
  • Buy 15 wrenches weekly (10 VIP store + 5 Glory shop)
  • Stop at 6 million — save remaining items for next week

Important: Using components doesn't give points. Only OPENING boxes gives points.

Day 2 (Tuesday): Shelter Upgrade

What gives points:

  • Construction upgrades
  • Construction speed-ups
  • Recruiting refugees (orange = 165k points)
  • Orange bounty missions (247k points each)

Strategy:

  • Save 20-40 refresh tickets — Use ONLY on Tuesday for orange missions
  • Refugee trick: Recruit once, then "Try Your Luck" to save tokens
  • When orange refugee appears — DON'T reset, recruit them
  • Save unfinished buildings for future upgrades
  • Sync with Full Preparedness at system time 8 and 20

Reality check: This is one of the HARDEST days. If you can't reach 6 million, stop at 3 million. Speed-ups are scarce.

Day 3 (Wednesday): Age of Science

What gives points:

  • Research speed-ups
  • Tech points
  • Consuming badges
  • Orange intercity trade trucks (4 trucks = 1.2 million points)

Strategy:

  • Use research speed-ups ONLY on Wednesday
  • Use badges ONLY on Wednesday
  • Complete 4 orange intercity trades = 1.2M points (easy)
  • Focus on Age of Science at system time 8 and 20

Reality check: Another hard day. Stop at 3 million if resources are low — at least get one versatile fragment.

Day 4 (Thursday): Hero Initiative

What gives points:

  • Hero fragments (any color)
  • Exclusive equipment fragments (orange only)
  • Primary recruitment
  • Power cores (equipment upgrade)
  • Equipment upgrades
  • Orange skill books

Strategy:

  • Easiest day to score!
  • Focus on orange gear upgrades first
  • Upgrade main 5 heroes equipment ONLY
  • Upgrade blue/purple heroes ONLY on Thursday (for gathering speed skills)

Critical warning: Do NOT spend enhancement alloys on purple/blue equipment. Save all alloys for orange equipment only.

Where to get Power Cores: Tent event rewards (up to 250), Military shop (1,000 — only after all 5 heroes have orange equipment).

Day 5 (Friday): Holistic Growth

What gives points:

  • Any speed-up (construction, research, training)
  • Training/assembling/promoting units
  • Radar tasks

Strategy:

  • Main focus: Training troops
  • Use training speed-ups ONLY on Friday
  • Save radar tasks from Thursday
  • Keep training daily, but save speed-ups for Friday

Warning: Don't train extra troops after Saturday battle losses. You might lose them and have nothing to train next Friday.

Day 6 (Saturday): Enemy Buster

What gives points:

  • Defeating enemy units
  • Your units getting killed in battle
  • Orange wanted missions
  • Orange intercity trade

Strategy:

  • Easiest event!
  • Ask enemies for tank vs tank battles
  • Or just attack and destroy
  • AVOID using bounty refresh tickets — save them for Tuesday

Tip: If low on points, just go kill. It's the simplest solution.

Weekly Rewards Potential

  • With maxed Alliance Recognition: ~8,000 badges per week
  • Without max research: Much less — prioritize this research!
  • Calculation: 830 medals per day × 2 (double rewards) × 5 days = 8,300 badges
  • Note: Building and Research days might only give 3M points, reducing total slightly

Key Takeaways

  • Save resources — Spend only on the right days
  • Sync with Full Preparedness — System time 8 and 20
  • Prioritize Alliance Recognition research — Double rewards, more badges
  • Don't chase 6 million blindly — Stop at 3 million if resources low
  • Be patient — This is a long-term grind, not a sprint
  • Refresh tickets — Save for Tuesday (Shelter Upgrade) only
  • Enhancement alloys — Orange equipment only, never purple/blue

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most important research for Alliance Duel?

Alliance Recognition research. It doubles your daily rewards (badges, alloys, fragments) and unlocks chests 7-9. Requires about 143-200k badges to fully max.

When should I use speed-ups?

Construction speed-ups on Day 2 (Tuesday). Research speed-ups on Day 3 (Wednesday). Training speed-ups on Day 5 (Friday). Never use speed-ups randomly.

What is Full Preparedness sync?

Full Preparedness events run at system time 8 and 20. Do your upgrades during these times to score in both Alliance Duel AND Full Preparedness simultaneously.

Should I always aim for 6 million points?

No. Don't chase 6 million blindly. If you lack resources, stop at 3 million. Especially true for Day 2 (building) and Day 3 (research) — the hardest days.

How many badges can I get per week?

With maxed Alliance Recognition (double rewards), around 8,000 badges per week if you complete most daily chests.

Which day is easiest/hardest?

Easiest: Day 4 (Hero Initiative) and Day 6 (Enemy Buster). Hardest: Day 2 (Shelter Upgrade) and Day 3 (Age of Science) — speed-ups are scarce.
