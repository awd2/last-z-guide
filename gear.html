How to Enhance Gear

Enhancement (Alloy)

Increases gear level using Enhancement Alloy. Access via Heroes ‚Üí Equipment ‚Üí Enhance.

Level caps by gear state:

  • Base orange gear: Level 50 max
  • Each completed hexagon: +5 levels (55, 60, 65, 70, 75)
  • Mythic transformation: +25 levels (max 100)

Important: You must reach level 20 before you can unlock promotion. Always enhance to level 20 first, then start promoting.

Promotion (Hexagons)

Orange gear has 5 hexagons, each with 6 steps = 30 total promotion steps.

Promotion uses:

  • Power Cores ‚Äî all steps
  • Zent (money) ‚Äî all steps
  • Orange equipment ‚Äî hexagons 4 and 5 only

First promotion step is HUGE: Only costs 100 Power Cores + 1M Zent but gives massive stats. Always prioritize getting all gear to at least 1-star (first step).

Optimal Upgrade Strategy

  1. Merit Shop: Buy orange equipment choice box EVERY week until all 5 heroes have full orange (20 pieces total)
  2. Enhance to 20: Level up all orange gear to level 20 (unlocks promotion)
  3. First hexagon first step: Promote ALL gear to 1-star (first step of hexagon 1)
  4. Complete hexagon 1: Finish all 6 steps of hexagon 1 on ALL gear
  5. Continue evenly: Hexagon 2 on all gear, then hexagon 3, then hexagon 4
  6. Stop at hexagon 4: Hexagon 5 costs nearly HALF of total resources ‚Äî skip unless whale

DON'T: Rush one piece to hexagon 5 while others are at hexagon 1. Power per resource is much better when upgrading evenly across all pieces.

First Step Efficiency

The very first promotion step (hexagon 1, step 1) costs only:

  • 100 Power Cores
  • 1M Zent

This gives MASSIVE stat boost for minimal cost. Always get all gear to this point first.

Hexagon 5 Warning

Hexagon 5 alone costs:

  • 12,800 Power Cores (50% of total cores)
  • 18.8M Zent (28% of total)
  • 6 orange items (60% of total items)

Unless you're a whale, stop at hexagon 4. The power gain per resource drops dramatically at hexagon 5.

Mythic Equipment (Whale Territory)

Mythic transformation is the ultimate gear upgrade ‚Äî but extremely expensive.

Skip mythic completely. The cost is astronomical for F2P players. Focus on getting all gear to 4 hexagons instead. Mythic is only for heavy spenders.

FAQ

What is the best / mythic equipment in Last Z?

Orange (legendary) gear is the best. It can be promoted through 5 hexagons and enhanced to level 75 (or 100 with mythic). For DPS heroes, prioritize Attack %. For tanks, prioritize HP %. Focus on your main 5 heroes only.

Should I upgrade purple gear?

No. Purple gear caps at level 30 with NO promotion. Never spend alloy or Power Cores on purple, blue, or green gear ‚Äî it's wasted resources. Convert all non-orange gear to alloy immediately.

How many hexagons should I complete?

For F2P: Stop at 4 hexagons. Hexagon 5 costs 12,800 Power Cores (50% of total) and 6 orange items (60% of total). The efficiency drops dramatically. Only whales should complete hexagon 5.

How do I use Power Cores in Last Z gear?

Enhance to level 20 first ‚Äî this unlocks promotion. Then do the first promotion step on all gear (100 cores + 1M zent each). The first step gives massive stats for minimal cost.

How much does full gear promotion cost?

Per piece: 25,400 Power Cores + 68M Zent + 10 orange items for all 5 hexagons. Total for 20 pieces (5 heroes): 508,000 Power Cores + 1.36B Zent + 200 orange items.

What is Mythic equipment?

Mythic transformation nearly doubles gear power and raises max level from 75 to 100. Requires at least 1 hexagon completed. Cost: $600-$1,200 per piece. F2P should skip it entirely.
