What is Lucky Discounter?

Lucky Discounter is a recurring event (every ~4 weeks) where you can purchase items at random discounts. The key is that certain attempts guarantee 90% off, making this the best event for farming badges.

Why badges matter: Badges are required for Alliance Recognition research, which unlocks medal production, hero training speed, and other critical bonuses. You need tens of thousands of badges to complete this research tree.

Strategy: Save tickets until you have enough to reach at least the 9th attempt (two guaranteed 90% discounts).

Price Comparison: Lucky Discounter vs VIP Shop

Badges

  • VIP Shop: 100 badges for 150 diamonds = 1.5 diamonds/badge
  • Lucky Discounter 90%: 2,000 badges for 600 diamonds = 0.3 diamonds/badge
  • Savings: 5x better value at 90% discount

Ticket Saving Strategy

Refresh tickets accumulate between events. The optimal approach:

Minimum Viable (9 tickets)

  • Guarantees two 90% discounts (5th and 9th)
  • Good for F2P with limited patience
  • Accumulates in ~2 months

Recommended (18 tickets)

  • Guarantees three 90% discounts (5th, 9th, 18th)
  • Best balance of waiting vs rewards
  • Accumulates in ~4-5 months

Maximum Value (32 tickets)

  • Guarantees four 90% discounts (5th, 9th, 18th, 32nd)
  • Requires ~8 months of saving
  • Best for patient endgame players

Tip: You get roughly 4-5 tickets per event cycle. Save them until you hit your target number.

Step-by-Step Strategy

1. Accumulate Tickets

Don't use refresh tickets when you get them. Save until you have at least 9 (ideally 18 or 32).

2. Prepare Diamonds

Before the event, collect diamonds from all sources. You'll need 30,000-80,000 depending on how many tickets you have.

3. Use Free Attempt First

Always start with the free attempt. Check the discount before deciding to buy.

4. Skip Low Discounts

If you get 10% or 30%, just refresh and move on. Don't waste diamonds on poor rates.

5. Buy Everything at 90%

When you hit guaranteed 90% attempts (5th, 9th, 18th, 32nd), buy ALL badges and speedups available.

6. Buy Badges at 70%

70% is still excellent value for badges. Purchase badges but skip speedups at this rate.

7. Optional: 50% Badges

If you have excess diamonds and need badges urgently, 50% is acceptable. Never buy speedups at 50%.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Buying speedups at 50% or less: VIP shop is the same price or cheaper
  • Using tickets immediately: Save them for bulk purchasing
  • Buying at 10-30% discount: Terrible value, always skip
  • Not preparing diamonds: You need diamonds ready when 90% hits
  • Ignoring the event: This is THE best source of cheap badges

Why Focus on Badges?

Badges fuel Alliance Recognition research, which provides:

  • Medal production (for hero upgrades)
  • Hero training speed bonuses
  • Alliance buffs and bonuses
  • Critical late-game power multipliers

You'll need 50,000-100,000+ badges to complete Alliance Recognition research. Lucky Discounter is the fastest F2P path to accumulating them. Use badges strategically during Alliance Duel Day 3 for maximum value.

FAQ

How often does Lucky Discounter appear?

Approximately every 4 weeks. Plan your ticket saving around this schedule.

Do refresh tickets expire?

No, tickets carry over between events. You can save them for months.

Should I buy training speedups?

Only at 90% discount. Training speedups are less critical than building/research speedups.

What if I don't have enough diamonds when 90% appears?

This is why preparation matters. Collect diamonds before the event starts. Missing a 90% discount is a significant loss.

Is 50% discount worth it for badges?

Yes, if you have lots of diamonds and urgently need badges. It's still better than VIP shop prices.
