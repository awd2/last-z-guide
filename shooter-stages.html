Last Z Shooter Stages Guide — Dodge & Shoot Tips

Beginner's Guide • Updated February 3, 2026 • 6 min read

By Last Z Guides

Quick Answer

Shooter stages are the Dodge & Shoot levels of Last Z. You dodge waves of zombies, shoot hazards, break barrels or gates for bonuses, and finish stages to earn rewards like hero XP from XP crates and other stage bonuses.

ℹ️ Best use: Clear stages regularly to keep rewards flowing and base growth moving.

Shooter Stages Basics

What Are Shooter Stages?

Shooter stages are the Dodge & Shoot portion of Last Z: Survival Shooter. They are vertical, scrolling levels packed with zombies, obstacles, and loot. Your goal is to dodge threats, shoot hazards, and reach the end of each stage to claim rewards and unlock further progress.

The official game description frames Dodge & Shoot as evading waves of undead while firing back for rewards. It is one of the core loops of the game and ties directly into exploration and expansion.

Barrels

Optional targets that can drop bonuses like extra troops, weapon upgrades, or buffs. If a barrel needs too much damage, it’s okay to bypass it.

Gates

Numbered gates that require damage to deplete. When reduced to zero, they grant large bonuses such as fully charged skills or troop boosts. Always weigh reward vs. firepower cost.

XP Crates

Stage-end rewards that grant hero experience, which you can invest into your main heroes.

Indestructible Hazards

Stage hazards like spikes or rolling wheels are indestructible and deal heavy damage — prioritize dodging them.

Why Shooter Stages Matter

Shooter stages are not isolated content. The shooter mode and base-building mode feed into each other: stages give rewards, and shelter growth sustains your overall progression loop.

Use stages as a steady progression engine alongside your base-building loop. If you’re pushing HQ, keep stages moving too — they are linked. For base priorities, see the HQ Upgrade Guide and Resources Guide.

Stage Strategy (Safe Defaults)

  • Always dodge hazards: Indestructible obstacles deal heavy damage, so your first priority is avoiding them.
  • Be selective with barrels: Break them when the payoff is good; skip them if they waste too much damage.
  • Evaluate gates: Gates can be powerful, but only if you can afford the damage cost.
  • Finish the stage: XP crates and rewards are delivered at stage end, so reaching the finish line matters.

FAQ
