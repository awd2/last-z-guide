How to Start Playing Last Z: Survival Shooter

Beginner's Guide • Updated January 2026 • 5 min read

Quick Answer

To start playing Last Z effectively: Complete the tutorial for free resources, focus on main quests to unlock features, join an active alliance for bonuses, upgrade your Command Center first, and save your diamonds for important events. These five steps will set you up for long-term success.

Step-by-Step Beginner's Guide

Step 1: Complete the Tutorial

The tutorial teaches you basic controls, combat mechanics, and base building. Don't skip it — completing the tutorial gives you free resources, heroes, and unlocks essential features. Takes about 10-15 minutes.

Step 2: Focus on Main Quests

The main quest line (shown on the left side of your screen) is your roadmap. Following it unlocks new buildings, heroes, and game modes. Side activities like exploration can wait until you've established your base.

Step 3: Join an Active Alliance

Alliances provide huge benefits: resource help, alliance gifts, group events, and protection. Look for an alliance with active members (check recent activity). Don't stay in a dead alliance — switch if needed.

Step 4: Upgrade Your Base

Your Command Center limits everything else. Prioritize it, then focus on resource buildings (farms, sawmills). A strong economy early means faster growth later. Use speed-ups wisely on long upgrades. See our HQ Upgrade Guide for details.

Step 5: Save Premium Currency

Diamonds are valuable. Don't waste them on speeding up timers or random chests. Save for: guaranteed hero summons, important events like Lucky Discounter, and VIP upgrades. Patience pays off.

Pro Tips for New Players

  • Log in daily — even 5 minutes to collect rewards compounds over time
  • Complete daily and weekly missions for consistent resource income
  • Don't upgrade all heroes — focus on 5-6 main heroes first (see Hero Tier List)
  • Keep your hospital capacity high to minimize troop losses
  • Scout before attacking anything in the world map

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Last Z: Survival Shooter free to play?

Yes, Last Z is free to download and play. It has optional in-app purchases, but you can progress without spending money. Many top players are free-to-play.

What should I focus on as a beginner in Last Z?

Focus on completing the tutorial, following main quests, joining an alliance, and upgrading your base. Avoid spending premium currency early — you'll need it later.

How do I get free diamonds in Last Z?

Complete daily tasks, achievements, events, and main quests. Join an active alliance for gift rewards. Watch ads when available. Events often give diamonds as milestone rewards.
