How to Level Up Fast in Last Z: Survival Shooter

Progression Guide • Updated January 2026 • 5 min read

Quick Answer

To level up fast in Last Z: Complete all daily tasks every day, focus on main campaign progression, always prioritize Command Center upgrades, participate in every event, and join an active alliance. Consistency beats intensity — 30 minutes daily is better than 5 hours once a week.

Fastest Leveling Strategies

Strategy 1: Never Miss Daily Tasks

Daily tasks are your bread and butter. They provide consistent XP, resources, and premium currency. Complete all of them every single day — the rewards compound over time and the XP adds up significantly.

Strategy 2: Push Main Campaign

The main campaign gives massive XP rewards for completing chapters. It also unlocks new features, buildings, and game modes. Don't get stuck farming early stages — push as far as you can.

Strategy 3: Command Center Priority

Your Command Center level caps everything else. Always have it upgrading. Use speed-ups on long Command Center upgrades to unlock new buildings and raise your power ceiling faster.

Strategy 4: Event Participation

Events offer 2-3x normal rewards plus exclusive items. Check events daily and complete as many milestones as possible. Even partial participation beats skipping entirely.

Strategy 5: Alliance Benefits

Active alliances provide: help with building/research timers, alliance gifts, group event rewards, and resource assistance. A good alliance can cut your progression time by 30-40%.

Resource Efficiency Tips

  • Don't spread upgrades across all heroes — focus on your main 5-6
  • Use speed-ups only on upgrades longer than 8 hours
  • Save premium currency for events with guaranteed rewards
  • Keep builders busy 24/7 — idle builders = wasted time
  • Check the shop daily for discounted resource packs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I level up fast in Last Z: Survival Shooter?

Complete daily tasks, progress through main campaign, prioritize Command Center upgrades, participate in events, and join an active alliance. Consistency is key — play a little every day.

What is the fastest way to get stronger in Last Z?

Focus resources on your main heroes, not all heroes. Upgrade Command Center first. Complete daily tasks consistently. Use resources efficiently on high-impact upgrades rather than spreading thin.
