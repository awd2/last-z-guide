Last Z F2P Guide — Best Diamond Spending, Free Sources, and What to Buy First

F2P Strategy Guide • Updated February 1, 2026 • 12 min read

By Last Z Guides

Best F2P plan for most players: buy permanent progression unlocks first, unlock all builders, invest in refugees for long-term speed, always keep shield diamonds by Friday, and only spend aggressively when events improve the value.

Quick Answer

Best Last Z F2P spending priority: key licenses first, then all 4 builders, then refugee tickets for Butlers and Scientists, while always keeping 2,000 diamonds by Friday for shields and saving big purchases for discounted events.

  1. ① Key Licenses · permanent unlocks (Strategy, Modification, Research)
  2. ② All 4 Builders · dramatically faster progression
  3. ③ Refugee Tickets · fastest growth boost (Butlers, Scientists)
  4. ④ Weekly Shields · keep 2,000 diamonds by Friday
  5. ⑤ Lucky Discounter · 40%+ off speedups every ~4 weeks

💡 Core rule: F2P progress comes from spending diamonds on permanent account growth, not on random convenience purchases.

🛡️ Friday rule: always keep 2,000 diamonds ready for weekly shield protection.

🎯 Best growth buy: refugees, builders, and permanent licenses usually beat short-term speedups.

How to Get Free Diamonds in Last Z

F2P diamond income comes from many small and medium sources rather than one jackpot system. The strongest free-to-play accounts stay consistent with arena, events, Furylord, gift codes, and long-term chest value instead of relying on random luck alone.

Daily Sources

Source Diamonds Notes
Furylord Achievements Varies Damage milestones unlock rewards
Diamond Nodes Varies World map exploration
Mission Chests Varies Chapter completion, daily tasks

Weekly/Event Sources

  • Arena Rank Rewards: Gold or higher = 1k-5k diamonds weekly
  • SVS Personal Boxes: Diamonds in boxes 1-3 (see SVS Guide)
  • Alliance Duel: Rewards for completing daily chests
  • Gift Codes: Active codes provide free diamonds (see Redeem Codes)
  • Event Milestones: Most events reward diamonds at certain thresholds

Furylord Tips

Furylord spawns 4 times daily (00:00, 06:00, 12:00, 18:00) except Sundays:

  • Use all 4 attacks daily — Even without fuel, spend fuel cans (you get 5 back from rewards)
  • 3M damage goal — Unlocks full purple A-tier gear from achievements
  • Faction bonus: Wait for your faction's 50% damage day to maximize achievements
  • Only highest single hit counts — Focus on one big hit, not multiple small ones

Best Diamond Spending Priority for F2P

If you are wondering what to buy first as F2P, the best answer is long-term account growth. Permanent unlocks, extra builders, and refugees improve every future week of progression, while random convenience spending usually disappears without lasting value.

Tier 1: Essential Purchases

1. Key Licenses (License Store)

  • Strategy License, Advanced Modification License, Research License
  • Permanent unlock — one-time purchase, lifetime benefits
  • Provides continuous extra resources and speedups
  • Priority: Buy as soon as you can afford them

2. All 4 Builders

  • Unlock all four workers for construction
  • Dramatically improves construction and research efficiency
  • One of the best long-term investments

3. Refugee Recruitment Tickets

  • THE fastest way to boost construction and research speed
  • Target: Butlers (construction) and Scientists (research)
  • Refugees provide permanent speed bonuses
  • Top priority for early game — see the Refugees Guide for ticket sources and target priorities

4. Weekly Shields

  • Always have 2,000 diamonds minimum by Friday
  • Alternative: Buy shields from Alliance store
  • Losing resources during Enemy Buster sets you back weeks
  • Protection is more valuable than spending on upgrades

Tier 2: High Value Purchases

Lucky Discounter Speedups

  • Event runs every 4 weeks
  • Buy speedups at 40%+ discount only
  • Building and research speedups are priority
  • See our Lucky Discounter Guide

VIP Shop Discounted Speedups

  • Check VIP shop for discounted speedups
  • Only buy when discount is significant

Best Shops for F2P Players

Shops matter because they convert limited currencies into long-term account power. For F2P players, the best shop is not always the flashiest one — it is the one that consistently turns your weekly routine into progress you cannot easily replace elsewhere.

VIP Shop

Buy:

  • Golden Wrenches — Essential for vehicle mods, peaks during Vehicle Boost events
  • Discounted Speedups — When available at good prices

Skip:

  • Power Cores (outside events) — Get up to 500 weekly free from Tyrant event
  • Full-price speedups

Time-Limited Shop

Buy:

  • All Badges — Crucial for high-tier upgrades
  • Versatile Orange Fragments — Unlocks at VIP 10, premium value
  • Gem-purchased items with good value

Skip:

  • Items with poor diamond-to-value ratio

Glory Shop

Always Buy:

  • Versatile Orange Hero Fragments
  • Golden Wrenches (5 weekly)
  • Equipment Framework Fragments (when available)
  • Random Component Boxes (when available)

Merit Shop

Note: Merit Shop is always available. Valor Medals (currency) are earned from SVS, Saturday Buster Day (Alliance Duel), and Canyon Clash.

Buy:

  • Power Cores — Priority during events
  • Versatile Orange Fragments

Skip:

  • Purple Equipment Boxes — Poor ROI
  • Basic Resource Crates — Low value

Growth Strategy

Growth-First Approach

One F2P player reached level 28 spending only $3 with this strategy:

  • First 4 months: ONLY invest in growth research
  • Skip military research early — Focus on construction/research speed
  • After growth maxed: Rapid troop advancement in just 2 months

Result: Slower early game, but catches up and surpasses players who spread resources thin.

Speed Boost Targets

Aim for 200% total boost through:

  • Villa upgrades — Construction speed
  • Bookstore upgrades — Research speed
  • Sophia (3-star) — Construction bonus (see Heroes)
  • Amelia — Research bonus
  • Refugees — Butlers (construction), Scientists (research)
  • Shelter research — Growth tree completion

When to Spend Diamonds for Maximum Value

One of the biggest differences between weak and strong F2P accounts is timing. Buying the right item during the right event often matters more than buying more items overall.

Check Calendar Before Buying

Items become significantly more valuable when paired with active events:

  • Golden Wrenches — Best during Vehicle Boost events
  • Speedups — Use during Alliance Duel days
  • Wrenches/Speedups — Score points in Full Preparedness
  • Power Cores — Merit Shop during SVS only

Alliance Duel Sync

Save resources for the right days (see Alliance Duel Guide):

  • Monday: Golden Wrenches, blueprints
  • Tuesday: Construction speedups
  • Wednesday: Research speedups, badges
  • Thursday: Hero fragments, power cores
  • Friday: Training speedups
  • Saturday: Combat — troop usage, attack events

F2P Survival Rules That Actually Matter

F2P success in Last Z is less about perfect luck and more about discipline. A few repeated rules — shield planning, event timing, focused spending, and avoiding waste — create most of the gap between stable growth and stalled accounts.

  • 2,000 diamonds by Friday — Shield money is sacred
  • Never use direct speedup button — Worst trade in the game
  • Refugees first — Butlers and Scientists are top priority
  • 4 builders ASAP — Dramatically speeds progression
  • Growth research before military — Patience pays off
  • Lucky Discounter = speedup time — Wait for 40%+ discounts

FAQ

What should I buy first with diamonds as F2P in Last Z?

Start with key licenses, then unlock all 4 builders, then invest in refugee recruitment for Butlers and Scientists, while always protecting a 2,000-diamond reserve for weekly shields.

How do I get free diamonds?

Free diamonds come from arena rewards, Furylord achievements, world map nodes, event milestones, and redeem codes. Consistent weekly collection matters more than one-time jackpots.

Should I buy speedups with diamonds?

Only during Lucky Discounter (40%+ discount) or VIP shop sales. Never use the direct speedup button. Save speedups for Alliance Duel days to double their value.

Why keep 2,000 diamonds for Friday?

For weekly shields during Saturday's Enemy Buster event. Without protection, losing resources sets you back weeks of farming. Shields are more valuable than any purchase.

What's the best shop for F2P?

Glory Shop offers best consistent value: Orange Hero Fragments, Golden Wrenches (5 weekly), Framework Fragments. Merit Shop is good during SVS for Power Cores and Orange Fragments.

Are refugees better than buying random speedups?

Usually yes. Refugees give permanent long-term account bonuses, while random speedups only solve a short-term timer and then disappear.

Can F2P players compete with spenders?

Yes, if they spend diamonds efficiently, time purchases around events, and focus on long-term growth systems instead of trying to match spender behavior directly.
