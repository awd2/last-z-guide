Hero Acquisition — Spending vs F2P

The Truth About Heroes

"The strongest hero in Last Z is your wallet." New heroes arrive via Battle Pass, requiring real money to unlock immediately.

However, F2P can still progress:

  • All heroes become available within 90 days of gameplay
  • Season heroes: Wait for season to end, then use Season Recruitment Tickets
  • Exclusive equipment: Usually free at the START of the next season (not immediately)
  • Smart hero selection and team composition can compete with spenders

Hero Tiers Explained

  • Orange heroes — Strongest, priority for resources
  • Purple heroes — Good alternatives if no orange heroes
  • Blue heroes — Support only (training, gathering), NOT for battle

Key insight: Even orange heroes without combat-focused 3rd skills are worth using because their 1st/2nd/4th skills deal 50%+ more damage than purple heroes.

Battle Formation Strategy

Formation Layout

Formation has 5 positions: 2 front row, 3 back row

    [Front 1] [Front 2]
  [Back 1] [Back 2] [Back 3]

Front row: Gets attacked first, needs to survive

Back row: Deals damage while front absorbs hits

Positioning Rules

  • Front row (2 heroes): Defense heroes with HP/defense buffs
  • Back row (3 heroes): DPS heroes with attack/damage buffs

How to identify:

  • Check hero tag: "Hero excels in strong defense" → Front row
  • Check hero tag: "Hero is good for DPS" → Back row
  • Check 3rd skill: HP/defense buffs → Front row
  • Check 3rd skill: Attack/damage buffs → Back row

Why This Matters

Example scenario:

  • Team A: Defense heroes front, DPS back (correct)
  • Team B: DPS heroes front, defense back (wrong)

Result: Team A wins. Their defense heroes absorb damage longer, while their DPS heroes keep dealing damage. Team B's DPS heroes die quickly because they can't absorb damage.

Key insight: Defense heroes have ~200k+ stamina and damage absorption. DPS heroes have high damage but die fast when attacked directly. For power optimization, see our Formation Power Guide.

Hero Skills Explained

Skill Structure (All Heroes)

  • 1st Skill: Normal Attack Boost — damage factor increase (similar across all heroes)
  • 2nd Skill: Active Skill — combat ability (similar damage factors, different animations)
  • 3rd Skill: Faction/Troop Buff — THIS IS THE DIFFERENTIATOR
  • 4th Skill: Faction Synergy — bonus when using same faction heroes

Key insight: 1st and 2nd skills are nearly identical across all orange heroes (1500 damage factor, 2400 active skill). The real difference is the 3rd skill.

3rd Skill Types

  • Combat buffs: Troop attack, defense, HP — BEST for battle
  • Support buffs: Construction speed, training speed, research — NOT for battle priority
  • Utility buffs: Exploration EXP, gathering speed — Situational

Priority: Heroes with combat-focused 3rd skills > Support 3rd skills

Exclusive Equipment

Season 1 heroes have exclusive equipment that:

  • Enhances 2nd skill damage significantly (+450-600 damage factor)
  • Adds attack, defense, and troop HP stats
  • Requires hero at 2-star to equip
  • Makes S1 heroes stronger than base S2 heroes

F2P: Exclusive equipment becomes free at the START of the next season.

Equipment Upgrade Strategy

Hexagon Upgrade Order

  1. Get ALL 5 main heroes to orange equipment first (Merit Shop weekly)
  2. Enhance ALL equipment to level 20 (unlocks promotion)
  3. Promote ALL equipment to 1-star (first hexagon step = 100 cores + 1M zent each)
  4. Complete hexagon 1 on ALL equipment
  5. Continue hexagon by hexagon evenly — stop at hexagon 4 (skip hexagon 5)

DON'T: Rush one piece to hexagon 5 while others are at hexagon 1. See our Gear Guide for full costs.

Why skip hexagon 5? It costs 12,800 Power Cores (50% of total) for only 20% more stats. Terrible efficiency.

Where to Get Orange Equipment

  • Merit Shop: 1 orange equipment choice box per week — buy EVERY week until all 20 pieces
  • Arena Shop: Orange equipment boxes
  • Exploration: Orange equipment at higher stages
  • Bullseye Event: Random chance for orange equipment

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the strongest faction in Last Z?

Wings of Dawn is currently strongest because it counters Blood Rose, which 95% of players use. For new players, Wings of Dawn gives you advantage in most battles.

What are the best heroes in Last Z?

Season 4 top heroes by faction: Blood Rose — Yu Chan S4 (S+ tier, massive damage), Licia S3, Bella S2, Selena S1. Wings of Dawn — Queenie S4 (S+ tier), Liliana S3, Alma S2. Guard of Order — Amber S4 (S+ tier), Dodemeki S3, Harleyena S2. Season 4 heroes are almost mandatory for competitive play.

Which troops should I use with each faction?

Blood Rose = Assaulter troops. Wings of Dawn = Shooter/Sutor troops. Guard of Order = Rider troops. Hero 3rd skills boost specific troop types. At 5-star, bonuses apply to all troops.

How should I position heroes in formation?

Front row (2 positions): Defense heroes with HP/defense buffs. Back row (3 positions): DPS heroes with attack/damage buffs. Check hero tags: "strong defense" = front, "strong DPS" = back.

Can F2P players get all heroes?

Yes. All heroes become available within 90 days. Season heroes require waiting for season end, then using season recruitment tickets. Exclusive equipment usually free at next season start.

Should I use purple heroes?

Only if you don't have enough orange heroes. Best purple heroes by faction: Blood Rose — Ava (B+, support), Miranda (B, frontline). Wings of Dawn — Christina (A, support stays useful), Fiona (B+, early carry). Guard of Order — Maria/Elizabeth/Leah (B, temporary F2P options). Purple heroes are temporary; replace with orange when possible.
