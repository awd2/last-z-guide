What Should You Do Before Season 2?

Use the season timing mainly as a planning checkpoint. If you want a farm account on your main server, create it before Season 2 starts because community reports say new same-server farm creation may be restricted after Season 2 begins.

For normal account progression, do not over-focus on the name change. Keep building your account foundation: join an active alliance, push HQ efficiently, save diamonds for long-term value, and follow the current in-game season screen.

When to Trust Older Season Guides

Older guides can still be useful for general preparation ideas, but they are risky when they use old season labels as if they still apply to every server. The safer approach is to use them for broad context only and confirm the current season name in-game.

If an older guide says "Season 2 Desert" while your server shows Winter, follow the Winter naming and avoid making plans that depend on Desert-specific assumptions.
