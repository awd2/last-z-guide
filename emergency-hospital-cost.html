FAQ

What does Emergency Hospital capacity do?

Capacity is the maximum number of injured troops the Emergency Hospital can hold. If the capacity is full, extra injured troops die.

What does upgrading Emergency Hospital increase?

Each upgrade increases hospital capacity and the building’s power value.

When does the Emergency Hospital unlock?

It unlocks at HQ level 14 (Building requirement: 2) after clearing Block XX.

Why are some resource values missing at higher levels?

The source list includes capacity and power for those levels but does not provide resource costs.
