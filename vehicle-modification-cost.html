Expert Modifier Strategy
  1. The Expert Modifier does not activate automatically when you unlock Hercules. You must enable it manually by clicking it in the top-left corner, in the same Modify section where you use wrenches. After unlocking Hercules, the Expert Modifier will glow to show it is available. It activates immediately when you click it. You can delay activation, but be very careful not to click it by mistake, because once enabled, it cannot be deactivated.
  2. Expert Modifier lasts 30 minutes and applies to 100 upgrades only. If you plan to use it right after unlocking Hercules, prepare at least 170k–200k blueprints to be safe. That should cover roughly 100 upgrades (about 20 levels). If you plan to use the Expert Modifier later, use this formula to estimate how many blueprints to save: current vehicle upgrade cost ×130. Example: if your current upgrade cost is 2,000 blueprints, prepare around 260k blueprints.
Tips and Tricks
  1. Unlock the Mia hero as early as you can, build her to 5 stars, and raise her 3rd skill to level 30 to reach her full potential.
  2. Pick up the Advanced Mod License from the License Store when possible. It saves thousands of blueprints early, and later can save hundreds of thousands of blueprints.
  3. Schedule vehicle upgrades for Alliance Duel – Modification Day (Mondays) and during Full Preparedness to maximize returns on golden wrenches and blueprints.

FAQ

What does the Expert Modifier do in Last Z?

Expert Modifier is a time-limited boost that applies to a fixed number of upgrades. It activates manually after unlocking Hercules and affects your next 100 upgrades during its 30-minute window.

What is Modification Rating / Elite modifier in Last Z?

Modification Rating is the modifier tier shown at each level. The Elite modifier labels in the table indicate those rating breakpoints.

How many wrenches are needed to unlock Hercules?

You unlock Hercules at level 95. The cumulative total to that milestone is 1,670 wrenches.

How many wrenches are required for Destroyer and Destroyer EX?

Destroyer unlocks at level 145 with a cumulative total of 10,380 wrenches. Destroyer EX unlocks at level 195 with a cumulative total of 35,240 wrenches.

What is Advanced Mod License in Last Z?

Advanced Mod License is a License Store item that saves blueprints on vehicle modification upgrades. It's most valuable early and still useful later for large blueprint savings.

How to Read This Table

  • W/sub — Wrenches per sub-level
  • Sub-levels — Number of sub-levels at that level
  • Total wrenches — Wrenches needed to finish the level
  • Cumulative total — Wrenches required from level 0 to this level
  • Modification Rating — The modifier tier shown at each level
