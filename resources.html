Last Z Resources Guide — How to Farm Food, Wood, Steel, and Diamonds Fast

Resource Guide • Updated February 1, 2026 • 7 min read

By Last Z Guides

Best resource strategy for most players: gather on the world map, skip most resource production buildings, save chests for bulk openings, and treat steel and farm accounts as separate late-game systems.

Quick Answer

Best way to get resources fast in Last Z: send full marches to gather, prioritize daily tasks and bounty rewards, skip most resource buildings, save chests for bulk openings, and use farm accounts only when your main account starts hitting late-game shortages.

  1. ① Gather overnight · send all marches before sleeping
  2. ② Skip resource buildings · gathering is 10-50x better
  3. ③ Save chests · open 10k+ in bulk for 20k+ diamonds
  4. ④ Steel from Tyrant · enable "Engage Offline"
  5. ⑤ Daily essentials · trucks, bounty, tasks

💡 Key rule: world gathering is usually much stronger than passive resource buildings, so builder time should stay focused on progression requirements.

🪙 Steel source: Tyrant rallies and steel nodes matter more than normal resource flow once HQ upgrades get expensive.

📦 Diamond trick: bulk chest openings are one of the main free-to-play ways to spike diamond income.

What Resources Matter Most in Last Z?

Not all resources matter in the same way. Food, wood, and electricity drive daily progression, steel becomes a major late-game bottleneck, and diamonds are your flexible premium resource for speeding up key account decisions.

Resource Used For Main Sources

Electricity Research, high-level upgrades Gathering, tasks, events
Steel HQ upgrades (25+), vehicles Tyrant, Steel nodes, events

Gold Daily shop purchases Tasks, events, exploration

Best Gathering Strategy for Fast Resources

If you want resources fast in Last Z, world gathering is the core system to optimize first. Full marches, overnight gathering, high-level nodes, and gathering bonuses do more for your economy than passive production buildings ever will.

Overnight Gathering

The most efficient way to get resources:

  1. Before sleeping, send ALL marches to gather
  2. Choose high-level nodes (more resources)
  3. Use heroes with gathering speed bonuses
  4. Fill marches completely — don't send half-empty
  5. Collect in the morning

Gathering Heroes

These heroes have gathering speed bonuses (keep at 4-star for global effect):

  • Giselle — Wood gathering speed
  • Athena — General gathering bonus
  • Blue heroes — Various gathering passives

Tip: You don't need to deploy gathering heroes — their 4th skill global effect works automatically.

Node Selection

  • Higher level nodes = more resources per hour
  • Closer nodes = less travel time
  • Alliance territory = gathering speed bonus
  • Avoid contested areas = risk of attack

Best Daily Resource Sources

The strongest resource income does not come from one source. The best accounts stack gathering, daily tasks, bounties, events, trucks, and alliance activity so no single shortage slows progression for long. If you want the routine behind this, use the Daily Checklist and the Events Schedule.

How to Get More Diamonds from Chests

Chests are a major source of diamonds, fragments, and rare items. Save them and open in bulk for better chances at rare drops.

Chest Type Possible Rewards Notes
Bounty Mission Chests Resources (90%), 100-5,000 diamonds, orange fragments Most common chest, 5k diamonds rare
Honor Chests Versatile exclusive equipment fragments From Alliance Duel, events
Presser Hunt Chests Speed ups, small diamonds Good for speed up stockpile
Hero Battlefield Honor Crate Up to 50 golden wrenches Wrenches are extremely rare
Dual Master Supply Box 10k diamonds, 3k badges (0.01%) Jackpot chest — very rare
Component Chests Level 1 component boxes (~8% drop) From various sources

Chest Opening Strategy

  • Save chests — don't open daily, accumulate for bulk opening
  • Open in bulk — better psychological feel, same odds but more chances
  • Bounty chests: ~90% are resource boxes, 10% have good rewards
  • Realistic expectations: 10k diamonds from 1,000+ chests is lucky

Real Example (from 10k+ chest opening)

  • ~20k diamonds total (including 10k and 5k jackpots)
  • ~40 universal orange hero fragments
  • 6k+ badges
  • 44 level 1 component boxes
  • Days worth of speed ups (construction, training, research)

Note: This required saving chests for weeks. Don't expect these results from daily opening.

How to Get Steel Fast

Steel is different from normal resource flow. Once HQ and late-game systems start demanding it, steel becomes a separate bottleneck that needs its own farming plan through Tyrant, nodes, and event sources. See the dedicated Steel Guide for a full breakdown.

1. Tyrant Rallies (Best Source)

  • Join alliance Tyrant rallies daily
  • Enable "Engage Offline" — auto-join rallies when you're not playing
  • Gives ~500+ Steel per week
  • Also gives Power Cores and other materials

2. Steel Nodes on World Map

  • Gather from Steel nodes (appear at higher levels)
  • Lower yield than Tyrant but always available

3. Events & Tasks

  • Alliance Duel rewards
  • Special events occasionally give Steel
  • Daily/weekly task rewards

Critical tip: Always enable "Engage Offline" for Tyrant. You get Steel passively without being online.

How to Get Diamonds

Diamonds are Diamonds. F2P can accumulate 20k+ diamonds from chest opening alone.

Regular Diamond Sources

  • Daily Tasks — Small but consistent
  • Achievements — One-time rewards
  • Events — Alliance Duel, Full Preparedness, special events
  • Arena Rankings — Top 50 gives extra rewards
  • Redeem Codes — Check our Codes page for free diamonds

Diamond Jackpots from Chests

Chest Type Diamond Reward Probability
Bounty Mission Chest 100 - 5,000 ~10% for any, 5k very rare
Dual Master Supply Box 10,000 0.01% (jackpot)
Presser Hunt Chest 100 - 1,000 Uncommon

Tip: Save bounty mission chests for weeks, then open thousands at once. One player got 20k diamonds from bulk opening (including 10k + 5k jackpots).

How to Spend Diamonds Wisely

  • DO: Save for Lucky Discounter (90% off) and Gacha Go events
  • DO: Buy VIP shop items (wrenches, badges)
  • DON'T: Speed up random timers
  • DON'T: Buy random chests (earn them instead)

How to Protect Resources from Raids

Warehouse Upgrades

Your Warehouse protects a portion of resources from raids:

  • Upgrade Warehouse to increase protection limit
  • Resources ABOVE the limit can be stolen
  • Keep resources below protection limit when offline

Protection Strategies

  • Spend before logging off — Start long upgrades/research
  • Use shields — When you have large amounts saved
  • Join NAP alliance — Non-Aggression Pact = less raids
  • Stay in alliance territory — Harder for enemies to reach
  • Don't hoard — Spend resources as you get them

When to Use Farm Accounts

Farm accounts are not an early-game requirement for most players. They become useful when your main account starts hitting regular resource shortages and you need extra long-term gathering support on the same server. For the full setup process, use the Farm Account Guide.

What is a Farm Account?

  • Secondary account focused only on gathering
  • Transfers resources to your main account
  • Essential for F2P players in late game

How to Set Up

  1. Create new account on same server
  2. Join same alliance as main
  3. Focus on gathering buildings and march capacity
  4. Send gathered resources to main via alliance transfers

Tips for Farm Accounts

  • Keep HQ low — no need to upgrade past gathering unlocks
  • Max gathering research
  • Build multiple farms if needed (1-2 is enough)
  • Don't invest in combat — only gathering

Should You Build Resource Buildings?

Many players waste builder time on food, wood, electricity, and steel production buildings. In most cases, these buildings are much weaker than map gathering and should stay at minimum levels unless an HQ requirement forces them higher.

Why Resource Buildings Are Bad

  • Terrible output — produce very little per hour
  • Gathering is 10-50x better — one gathering trip = days of production
  • Waste builder time — builders should focus on HQ prerequisites
  • Waste resources — upgrading them costs more than they produce

What Buildings to Skip

  • Farmhouse (food production)
  • Residence (wood production)
  • Wind Turbine (electricity production)
  • Smelting Plant (steel production)

Exception: Only build these if required for HQ upgrade prerequisites. Keep them at minimum required level.

FAQ

How do I get more resources fast in Last Z?

Use full marches for world gathering, complete daily tasks and bounty missions, run trucks, save chests for bulk openings, and add farm accounts later if your main account starts falling behind on resources.

Should I open chests daily or save them?

Save chests and open in bulk. Bounty mission chests give 90% resource boxes anyway. Saving thousands and opening at once gives more chances at rare drops (5k diamonds, 10k diamonds, fragments). One bulk opening can yield 20k+ diamonds.

Are resource buildings worth upgrading in Last Z?

Usually no, unless an HQ requirement forces them higher. Passive production is weak compared with gathering, so builder time is better spent on progression buildings.

What is the best way to get steel in Last Z?

For most players, Tyrant rallies are the main steel source, followed by steel nodes, events, and other late-game reward systems. Steel should be treated as a separate bottleneck from food, wood, and electricity.

What is a farm account in Last Z?

A farm account is a secondary account focused on gathering resources for your main account. It is most useful in later progression when normal income is no longer enough.

How do I get free diamonds?

Save bounty mission chests (can give 5k each), daily tasks, achievements, events, arena rankings, and redeem codes. Bulk opening 10k chests can yield 20k+ diamonds. Save diamonds for Lucky Discounter and Gacha Go events.
