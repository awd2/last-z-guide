Last Z PvP Guide — Faction Counters, Events & Pro Tips

PvP Guide • Updated January 2026 • 10 min read

Quick Answer: Win PvP by using faction counters (Wings of Dawn beats Blood Rose — the most common faction). Scout opponents before attacking (check kill count, not just power). Attack on Saturday when hospital capacity doubles. For events: Canyon Clash rewards teamwork over power, SVS requires state coordination, State Ruler can be kraken-dominated. Join a NAP alliance for protection.

The Faction Counter Triangle

This is the most important PvP knowledge in Last Z:

  • Wings of Dawn → defeats → Blood Rose
  • Guard of Order → defeats → Wings of Dawn
  • Blood Rose → defeats → Guard of Order

Why this matters: Most players focus on Blood Rose because it's the first faction released. By building Wings of Dawn, you counter the majority of players. A 120M Wings of Dawn formation can defeat a 130M Blood Rose formation.

Recommended Mixed Formation

  • 3 Wings of Dawn heroes — main faction for counter advantage
  • 2 Blood Rose heroes — for synergy bonuses

Season 3 heroes give damage resistance when deployed with Season 2 heroes, combining faction advantage with stat bonuses.

Battle Preparation

Scouting Your Opponent

Never attack blindly. Check these stats before engaging:

  • Kill count: Low kills = weak in actual combat (inflated power from buildings)
  • Hero power: Compare to yours
  • Modified vehicle power: Key combat stat
  • Tech power: Low tech = Alliance Recognition not maxed
  • Alliance: Avoid targets with strong alliance backup

Key insight: High total power with low kills means inflated power from buildings/research — easy target.

Hospital Strategy

Hospital capacity determines how many wounded troops you save.

  • Saturday bonus: Hospital capacity doubles during Enemy Buster and State Ruler
  • Example: 7,000 capacity → 14,000 on Saturday
  • Each hospital level 25: +1,000 capacity bonus

Research: Hospital Expansion (capacity) and Healing Speed in Rapid Growth tree.

Hero Selection for PvP

Pick heroes by their 3rd skill — first and second skills are similar across heroes.

  • Good 3rd skills: Troop attack/defense, HP, damage resistance
  • Bad 3rd skills: Free radar events, resource production, training speed only
  • Season priority: S3 > S2 > S1 (newer heroes have better combat skills)

Canyon Clash — Alliance PvP

Why Canyon Clash is the Best Event

Players agree: "PVP Events are why we play." Canyon Clash brings alliances together with strategy discussions and coordinated attacks. It's fun for everyone — not just whales.

Winning Tactics

  • Use voice comms — Discord coordination beats individual strength
  • Guerilla tactics — hit-and-run, target isolated enemies
  • Focus fire — concentrate attacks on one target at a time
  • Timing — coordinate simultaneous strikes

When Outmatched

  • Guerilla tactics still work
  • Target resource nodes, not direct fights
  • Make them work for every point
  • Learn from losses — watch replays

SVS (State vs State)

How SVS Works

SVS brings different servers together every two weeks for large-scale battles. Teamwork matters more than individual strength.

Preparation

  • Coordinate with all alliances in your state
  • Assign roles: attackers, defenders, scouts
  • Set rally points and timing
  • Save resources for SVS week

Capital Clash

Part of SVS — unlock all 9 boxes for maximum rewards. Requires state-wide coordination and planning.

State Ruler

The Kraken Problem

Honest assessment: Many State Ruler matches boil down to 1-2 players so absurdly strong that no tactics can overcome the power gap.

  • Some matches are complete massacres
  • Weaker states sometimes don't engage at all
  • Result: disappointment on both sides

What You Can Do

  • Focus on event points — not just wins
  • Avoid direct kraken fights — target other objectives
  • Coordinate with state — collective effort matters
  • Accept limitations — some fights aren't worth taking

Note: Zombie Siege and Tyrant kills don't count for State Ruler attacker rights.

Boss Fights

Furylord, Zombie Siege, Tyrant

  • Furylord: Switch factions based on daily rotation
  • Zombie Siege (ZS): Group content, coordinate with alliance
  • Tyrant: Enable "Engage Offline" for auto-join rallies, provides Steel

Chip System Planning

Chips unlock around end of Season 2. Plan ahead:

  • Each formation needs separate chips
  • Chips used in Formation 1 cannot be used in Formation 2
  • Orange quality chips provide resonance bonuses
  • Components unlock at modification level 150

Critical: Decide your main faction BEFORE chips arrive. Switching factions after investing in chips is extremely expensive.

Essential PvP Tips

  • Join a NAP alliance — protects your base from being farmed
  • Scout before attacking — check kills, not just power
  • Attack on Saturday — hospital capacity doubles
  • Get Urgent Rescue — Peace Shield research, -20% unit losses
  • Solo attacks waste troops — always coordinate with alliance
  • Save shields for overnight — protect resources while sleeping
  • Create farm accounts — resources become scarce late game
  • Upgrade purple heroes — Hall of Honor gives all-hero buffs

Glossary

  • NAP — Non-Aggression Pact (don't attack each other)
  • Kraken — Top spender/whale (extremely powerful player)
  • SVS — State vs State event
  • ZS — Zombie Siege boss event
  • Rally — Coordinated group attack
  • Farming — Repeatedly attacking weaker players for resources
  • Chips — Equipment system unlocked in Season 2

FAQ

Should I focus on Blood Rose or Wings of Dawn?

Wings of Dawn. Most players use Blood Rose because it's released first, so Wings of Dawn gives you a counter advantage against the majority of opponents.

How do I know if someone is weak despite high power?

Check their kill count. High power but low kills means inflated power from buildings/research without actual combat strength.

When should I attack during the week?

Saturday is best — hospital capacity doubles during Enemy Buster/State Ruler events, meaning fewer permanent troop losses.

What is Canyon Clash?

Alliance PvP event rewarding coordination over power. Use Discord voice comms and guerilla tactics. Even smaller alliances beat stronger ones with teamwork.

What is SVS?

Large-scale server vs server battles every 2 weeks. Teamwork matters most. Coordinate with all alliances in your state.

Why can't I beat krakens in State Ruler?

Power gaps are real. Some players spend $15k+ and can't be overcome with tactics. Focus on event points and objectives you can achieve.

Can I switch factions after investing in chips?

Technically yes, but extremely expensive. Each formation needs separate chips. Decide your faction before chip system arrives.
