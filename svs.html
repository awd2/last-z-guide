What is SVS?

Event Overview

SVS (State vs State) is one of the biggest competitive events in Last Z. Every two weeks, 4 servers are matched together for cross-state battles. Teamwork matters more than individual strength.

The event has two main components:

  • State Ruler Prep Phase â€” Sunday to Friday
  • Capital Clash â€” Saturday (main battle day)

Matchmaking System

4 servers are grouped for a 2-3 week battle until a champion is crowned:

  • Round 1: Random pairings among four servers
  • Round 2: Winners compete for 1st/2nd place
  • Round 2: Losers compete for 3rd/4th place
  • All servers get two matches regardless of Round 1 result

Preparation Phase (Sunday-Friday)

Why Prep Phase Matters

The server with higher prep phase points becomes the Invader. The lower-scoring server becomes the Defender.

Invader advantage: Freedom to teleport into enemy territory and control attack timing.

Defender must: Protect their capital; attacks primarily serve as counter-moves requiring fast coordination.

FAQ

What is SVS in Last Z?

SVS (State vs State) is a biweekly cross-server event where 4 servers compete in elimination format. Prep phase runs Sunday-Friday, Capital Clash happens Saturday.

How do I become the invader?

Earn more prep phase points than opponent server. Points come from Alliance Duel (1st place = 6,000), Arena Champion (20,000), Alliance Duel victory (30,000), Furylord damage, and truck raids.

What are Valor Medals used for?

Exchange Valor Medals in the Black Market (Merit Shop) for orange equipment. Sources: SVS (main), Saturday Buster Day, Canyon Clash. SVS 9 boxes provide ~45,000 Valor Medals.

Can I get rewards if my server loses?

Yes. Personal reward boxes are based on your individual points, not server performance. Focus on earning 2.25M total points to unlock all 9 boxes.

How often does SVS happen?

Every two weeks. Plan your resources and troop training around the SVS schedule.

What's the difference between invader and defender?

Invaders can teleport into enemy territory and control attack timing. Defenders must protect their capital and coordinate counter-attacks quickly.
